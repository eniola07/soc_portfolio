Alerts monitoring and response with SIEM and XDR

MS SC-900 / MS SC-200

In preparing for the Microsoft SC-900 and SC-200 exams, I explored extensive documentation and interactive guides that provided deep insights into the benefits of an integrated SIEM-XDR security solution in a modern SOC environment. This journey not only helped me build a strong theoretical foundation but also allowed me to test these solutions in real-world scenarios. In this writeup, I selected an interactive guide that showcases how Microsoft 365 Defender with XDR capabilities, along with Microsoft Sentinel, empowers organizations to monitor, detect, protect against, and respond to security threats and attacks.

Outcome

Upon completing the exercise, I gained a strong understanding of the typical responsibilities and day-to-day tasks of a Tier 1 SOC analyst.
